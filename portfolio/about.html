About

I am a digital curator with an interest in university presses and university libraries. I have a background in English and editorial work and recent training in library science and web development.

As a student earning the Master of Science in Information at the University of Michigan, I've assessed qualitative researchers' needs for library support, curated book metadata for an NEH/Mellon digitization project, and developed features for Harvard Digital Collections while interning at Harvard Library.
