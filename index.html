Bio

I am an editor, librarian, and developer working on books in the humanities and social sciences. Currently I am a Digital Publishing Coordinator at the University of Michigan Library, where I guide authors through the book production process, manage digital conversion and metadata enrichment projects for collections such as Michigan Asian Studies and ACLS Humanities E-Book, and conduct research into the needs of qualitative researchers through the Library Assessment for Research and Scholarship Lab.

In the past, I have developed and tested features for Harvard Digital Collections as an intern at Harvard Library, served as a Fulbright scholar to Poland at the University of Silesia in Katowice, and taught writing and rhetoric at the college level.

I hold an MS in Information from the University of Michigan, an MA in English from Georgetown University, and a BA in English from Millsaps College.
